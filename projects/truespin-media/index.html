Truespin Media is a small partnership that my friend, Nick Leeper, and I started up. Its basically just around so that we can split profits on side-projects. We originally planned on offering several services including: video production, photography, graphic design, and web design. However, we've focused primarily on the last 2.

v2 - latest version

After realizing that we were mainly focusing on web design and not the other services we offered, we decided to redesign the site. This version of our site is much lighter and contains only relevant information. With this release we also drastically updated our portfolio - with all the work we were doing, we didn't have enough time to update our own site!

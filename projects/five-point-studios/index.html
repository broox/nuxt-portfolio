Five Point Studios

September 2007
  • CSS
  • Graphic Design
  • HTML
  • MySQL
  • PHP
Five point studios is a professional piercing and custom tattoo shop in Johnston, IA. I was given the opportunity to revamp their severely outdated web site. I programmed the site so that the shop could add/edit any page as well as add/edit staff members and staff portfolio images. In the design I incorporated one of the shop's tattoo sketches. The shop also wanted a very artistic font in their design. And as with any web site, if the visitors don't have your font installed, they won't see your fancy text. So, using PHP's GD library, I wrote functionality to dynamically load their font and create fancy title images on the fly. The site will be much more successful in allowing Five Point to show off the incredible work that consistantly comes out of their shop.
